Some notes:

  • Watch the video first before reading the strat (so you can know the placements).
  • Skip until wave 20 as fast as possible (for better result).
  • Use EDJ ability wisely, it can help you pretty much.
  • Read the targetting guide and follow it.

Strat:

Preparation:

  • 1. Sniper
  • 2. Missile Trooper
  • 3. Barrack
  • 4. EDJ

Wave 1-19:

RAPIDLY SKIP UNTIL WAVE 20!

  • Spawn 4x 0-0 Barracks.
  • Upgrade 1 Barrack to level 3-0.
  • Place 2x 2-0 Snipers (one at a time) on the gray barrier on the right side of the map (facing the enemy spawn)
  • Upgrade the 3-0 Barrack to 4-2.
  • Place Missile Trooper in the x-cross area on the barrels, upgrade it to 2-2, and set it to strongest.
  • Upgrade another Barrack to 4-2.
  • Upgrade Missile Trooper to 3-2.
  • Upgrade another Barrack to 4-2.
  • Upgrade the last Barrack to 0-4.
  • Upgrade Missile Trooper to 5-2.
  • Upgrade 0-4 Barrack to 0–5.

Wave 20-27:

SKIP WAVE 20 AT 30-33 SECONDS LEFT!

  • Upgrade both Snipers to 5-2 and set them targeting to last.
  • Place a level 2-5 Missile Trooper.
  • Set a 3-2 EDJ on the right back cliffs (facing to your base).
  • Spam 2–5 Snipers around EDJ.
  • Use EDJ ability mid-wave 26 and max the EDJ when the ability runs out.
  • Use the second EDJ ability when the timer reaches 30 seconds.
  • Place a 5-2 EDJ and spam 2-5 Snipers there.

Final Wave:

  • Max Barracks (optional).
  • Set left cliff Snipers to strongest and a few from the right (facing to your base).
  • Spam EDJ abilities.
  • Spam 0-0 Snipers near the boss to bait attacks and slow him down.
  • Place a few 5-2 to kill Juggernauts.
  • When you run out of the cliff space spam more 2-5 Snipers on high ground or anywhere that can shoot the boss as much as possible for a better result.
